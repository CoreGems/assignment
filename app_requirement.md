i would like you to crate  a short UI/UX prototype . The goal of this exercise is to evaluate:

UI/UX design thinking
Front-end development skills
Branding and styling capabilities
Creativity in presenting business information
Attention to detail and user experience
This is intentionally scoped as a lightweight exercise and should not require more than 2–3 hours.

Use Case
Design a modern web-based portal for field/sales/service agents to track their incentives, performance, and engagement programs.

The application should provide users with a personalized and engaging experience to:

Track earnings and payouts
Monitor performance progress
View active incentive programs
Stay motivated and engaged
Target users may include:

Sales representatives
Field agents
Customer support/service teams
Retail/store associates
You are free to define the branding direction, layout, interactions, and overall experience.

Assignment Scope
Build:

1–2 responsive web pages/screens
Suggested screens:

Main Dashboard / Home
Incentive Details or Performance Breakdown page
Sample KPIs / Data Ideas
You may use mock/static data for items such as:

YTD Incentive Earnings
Monthly Bonus Projection
Active Incentive Programs
Performance Progress %
Ranking / Score
Points / Rewards
Goal Completion Status
Monthly Trend Charts
Achievement Milestones / Badges
Upcoming Payout Date
These are only suggestions — feel free to expand creatively.
